Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our Word to PDF converter

Is this Word to PDF converter free to use?

Yes, our Word to PDF converter is completely free to use with no hidden charges. You can convert as many files as you need without any watermarks or limitations.

How secure is my document during conversion?

Your documents are processed securely using 256-bit SSL encryption. All files are automatically deleted from our servers immediately after conversion, ensuring your privacy and security.

What Word file formats do you support?

We support both older .doc files (Microsoft Word 97-2003) and modern .docx files (Word 2007 and later). The converter preserves all formatting, images, tables, and other elements.

Is there a file size limit for conversion?

The maximum file size for conversion is 10MB. For larger files, we recommend compressing the document or splitting it into smaller parts before conversion.

Can I convert multiple Word files at once?

Currently, our free converter processes one file at a time. For batch conversions, consider our premium version which allows multiple file conversions simultaneously.

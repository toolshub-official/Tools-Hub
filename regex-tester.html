At RegexPro, we believe that regular expressions should be accessible to everyone. Our mission is to simplify the process of creating, testing, and debugging regex patterns through an intuitive, powerful online tool.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about RegexPro and regular expressions.

What is RegexPro?

RegexPro is an advanced online tool for testing, debugging, and learning regular expressions. It provides real-time feedback, detailed match information, and pattern explanations to help developers work with regex more effectively.

Is RegexPro free to use?

Yes, RegexPro is completely free to use with no hidden costs. We may offer premium features in the future, but the core functionality will always remain free.

Does RegexPro store my regex patterns?

RegexPro can store your recent patterns in your browser's local storage for convenience, but we don't send or store your patterns on our servers unless you explicitly choose to save them to your account (future feature).

What regex flavors are supported?

RegexPro uses JavaScript's RegExp implementation, which is similar to Perl-style regular expressions. Most common regex features are supported, including lookaheads, backreferences, and character classes.

How do I learn regular expressions?

We recommend starting with our Regex Basics tutorial and practicing with simple patterns. Our Cheat Sheet is also a great reference as you're learning.

Can I use RegexPro on mobile devices?

Yes! RegexPro is fully responsive and works on smartphones, tablets, and desktop computers. The interface adapts to your screen size for optimal usability.

How accurate are the regex explanations?

Our explanation system covers most common regex syntax accurately. For very complex patterns, some explanations might be simplified. We're continually improving our explanation engine.

Can I suggest a feature?

Absolutely! We welcome feature requests and feedback. Please use our contact form to share your ideas.

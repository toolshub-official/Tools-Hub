    Frequently Asked Questions

    How accurate are the conversions?

    Our conversions are highly accurate, using standard conversion factors recognized internationally. For temperature conversions, we use precise formulas that account for the different zero points of each scale.

    Is my conversion history stored?

    Your conversion history is stored locally in your browser using localStorage. We don't send this data to any servers. You can clear your history at any time using the "Clear History" button.

    Can I use this tool offline?

    Yes! After your first visit, the tool will be cached by your browser and you can use it offline. All conversion logic happens in your browser, no internet connection is needed for the conversions themselves.

    How do temperature conversions work?

    Temperature conversions are different from other units because they don't have a linear relationship. We use the following formulas:

    • Celsius to Fahrenheit: °F = (°C × 9/5) + 32
    • Fahrenheit to Celsius: °C = (°F - 32) × 5/9
    • Celsius to Kelvin: K = °C + 273.15

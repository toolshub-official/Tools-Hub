About ProCrop

ProCrop is a free, online image cropping tool designed to help you quickly and easily crop your images to the perfect size and aspect ratio. Whether you're preparing images for social media, a website, or personal use, ProCrop provides all the tools you need in a simple, intuitive interface.

Our Mission

We believe that image editing should be accessible to everyone, without the need for expensive software or complicated tools. Our mission is to provide high-quality, browser-based tools that are:

  • Free to use with no hidden costs
  • Simple and intuitive
  • Private - your images never leave your browser
  • Fast and responsive
  • Accessible on any device

Features

Multiple Aspect Ratios

Choose from standard ratios like 1:1, 16:9, and 4:3, or use freeform cropping.

Drag & Drop

Easily upload images by dragging and dropping them onto the page.

Live Preview

See exactly how your cropped image will look before downloading.

Dark Mode

Work comfortably in any lighting condition with our dark mode support.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 20, 2023

At ProCrop, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our website and services.

Information We Don't Collect

ProCrop is designed to process your images entirely in your browser. This means:

  • We do not upload your images to our servers
  • We do not store your images
  • We do not have access to your images
  • We do not collect any personal information through the image cropping process

Information We Do Collect

For analytics and improvement purposes, we may collect anonymous usage data such as:

  • Browser type and version
  • Device type
  • Operating system
  • Approximate geographic location (country level)
  • Pages visited and features used
  • Error reports (if you choose to send them)

Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to:

  • Remember your preferences (like dark mode settings)
  • Analyze site usage
  • Support advertising (through third-party services like Google AdSense)

You can control cookies through your browser settings.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may collect information:

  • Google Analytics: For understanding how visitors use our site
  • Google AdSense: For displaying relevant advertisements

These services have their own privacy policies which we encourage you to review.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
